## Featured Projects

Each project is documented end to end: problem statement, data, method, insight, and recommendation.

### Customer Churn Risk Analysis (Energy Sector)

**Problem:** Retention teams were contacting customers with no prioritisation, spending outreach budget on accounts that were never at risk.
**Method:** Cleaned and engineered features in Python, built and validated a classification model against a held-out set.
**Insight:** ROC-AUC of 0.919 across 12,400 meters, with churn risk concentrated in identifiable customer segments.
**Recommendation:** Rank all meters into risk tiers and direct retention outreach to the top tier first.

> **Tech Stack:** Python, scikit-learn, pandas

### Regulatory Compliance Gap Analysis (Manufacturing)

**Problem:** A manufacturer needed to know how ready their product data was for an incoming EU regulatory standard, ahead of the deadline.
**Method:** Built a weighted scoring pipeline in Python with fully reproducible, automated reporting across the seven required data sections.
**Insight:** Overall readiness of 60.3%, with four required sections showing zero data coverage.
**Recommendation:** A remediation plan prioritised by section weight, closing the highest-impact gaps first.

> **Tech Stack:** Python, pandas, automated reporting

### [NHS A&E Winter Performance Analysis](https://github.com/auwalmusa/nhs-ae-performance-analysis)

**Problem:** Which trusts deteriorated most under winter pressure, which recovered fastest, and where should improvement support be targeted before next winter?
**Method:** Reproducible Analytical Pipeline: tested Python cleaning stage, SQL analysis with CTEs and window functions, two-page interactive Power BI dashboard with trust drill-down.
**Insight:** National performance bottomed at 57.0% in January 2026 while 12-hour waits spiked 41%, with attendances 1.5% below the autumn average; volume-performance correlation of 0.004 shows a capacity problem, not a demand problem.
**Recommendation:** Target flow and capacity interventions; prioritise the South West and the trusts with the steepest January collapse.

> **Tech Stack:** Python, SQL, Power BI, NHS England open data

### SQL Deep Dive: UK Open Data (In Progress)

**Problem:** A business analysis built entirely in SQL, answering a defined set of commercial questions from a UK public dataset.
**Method:** Complex joins, window functions, and CTEs, with every query documented and results written up as a short business report.
