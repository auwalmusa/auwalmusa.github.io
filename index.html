Pythonscikit-learnClassification

Customer Churn Risk Analysis, Energy Sector

Problem
Retention teams were contacting customers with no prioritisation, spending outreach budget on accounts that were never at risk.
Data
Consumption and account history for 12,400 meters.
Method
Cleaned and engineered features in Python, built and validated a classification model, tested against a held-out set.
Insight
The model achieved ROC-AUC 0.919, with churn risk concentrated in identifiable customer segments.
Recommendation
Rank all meters into risk tiers and direct retention outreach to the top tier first, replacing untargeted contact.

PythonpandasAudit Analytics

Regulatory Compliance Gap Analysis, Manufacturing

Problem
A manufacturer needed to know how ready their product data was for an incoming EU regulatory standard, ahead of the deadline.
Data
Technical documentation mapped against the seven data sections required by the standard.
Method
Built a weighted scoring pipeline in Python with fully reproducible, automated reporting.
Insight
Overall readiness was 60.3%, with four required sections showing zero data coverage.
Recommendation
A remediation plan prioritised by section weight, closing the highest-impact gaps first.
